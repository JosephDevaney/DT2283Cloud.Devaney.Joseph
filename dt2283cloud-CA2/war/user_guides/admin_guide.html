You are logged in as an administrator. You can upload new images, delete any images, view all private images and download all images.

To upload a new image click here. You can then browse your computer for files to upload, all standard image formats are accepted via the Browse button. Choose whether you would like the picture to be public or private with the Private checkbox. The default will be Private. Finally, clicking on the upload button will submit your picture to PictureBox and you will be redirected to view all pictures.

To Delete or Download an image, you can click on the relating button beside the picture.

To logout click the Login/Logout button.
